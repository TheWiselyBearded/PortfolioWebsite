Why I Love Mixed Realities

As a child I was always consumed by my imagination. Days would be spent playing outside pretending as though I'm in some heroic world fighting off the enemies. Toys would be thrown, and lego sculptures would be built. I could only dream of a day where my actual reality would consist of such exciting imaginary worlds. That day has come. Let us fast forward from age 5 to 21. I now invested my money towards a custom build PC along with an HTC Vive. A few weeks ago, I spent my days and nights programming and recreating a Star Wars VR Simulator in Unity. It was always a childhood dream of mine to be capable of using the force. Thanks to Fuseman tutorials and dubious curiosity, I was achieving of doing this!
